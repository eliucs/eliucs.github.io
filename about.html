About Me.

You've probably been wondering, who am I, and what do I do?

My name is Eric Liu, I am a Computer Science and Business Administration student at the University of Waterloo, currently enrolled in the Double Degree (BCS/BBA) Program.

I am very passionate about self-teaching new concepts, working on interesting projects, and collaborating in teams. I think that constantly learning and scanning for new developments in the realm of computer science is crucial to keeping up-to-date with current and future technologies.

"Never stop learning, because life never stops teaching."

In my spare time, I work on a multitude of projects. Some are ongoing works-in-progress, like Differentio and Newstream, which both incorporate machine learning techniques, which are topics I'm currently exploring on my own.

Other times, I work on mini-projects and experiments, which I use to test and keep myself in check with important concepts with algorithms, object-oriented, dynamic and functional programming.

I've worked with many languages and frameworks: I'm fluent in Java, C/C++, Python, SQL, Javascript, HTML 5, CSS 3 and Scheme/Racket. I'm familiar with jQuery, Node, Express, Angular, MongoDB and Android Studio.
